About This Demo Orchestration Platform
Think of the agents as musicians and the orchestration platform as the conductor, bringing in the right musician at the right moment to create a seamless performance.

In this demo, the platform is connected to a single agent from a data provider called HarvinAds. In a full production environment, each data provider would build their own agent, and the orchestration platform would coordinate them all — ensuring the right agent is called at the right time to deliver the required data or capability.

Simply enter your ad brief, and the Signals Agent will:

Find matching segments from available providers
Propose additional AI-generated segments for consideration

This is a demo environment. In a production setup, you would also have the option to activate segments directly into your chosen destination platform.

The platform is powered by the AdCP protocol.
